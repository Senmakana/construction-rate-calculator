BUILDING CONSTRUCTION RATES

Introduction

Welcome to the Construction Rate Calculator This application is designed to help builders, contractors, and construction professionals quickly determine the cost of construction work per unit. Many construction tasks such as blockwork, plastering, and other masonry works are commonly measured in square meters. Instead of estimating materials manually, this tool allows you to calculate the rate per square meter based on the materials used and their current prices. With this tool you can: Enter the block size you are using. Specify the mix ratio of materials (such as cement and sand). Input current material prices. Automatically calculate the rate per square meter of the work. The goal is to simplify calculations, reduce estimation errors, and help contractors make faster and more accurate cost decisions on site.
